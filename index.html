About Me

Hello! My name is Alexa Calder, and I'm a Junior at Northeastern University studying Experience design. Art has always been my favorite subject–and my best. After a brief stint in third grade when I believed I was going to be a professional soccer player, then in fourth, an investment banker, when asked what I wanted to be when I grew up, I said an artist. Or a designer. Or an interior decorator. It wasn’t long before my room became a center of creativity for me. Every few months, I’d be inspired, spending a weekend in a frenzy of activity, painting my walls different colors, rearranging my furniture, installing both a swing and hammock, and covering my walls in a variety of paint and pencil. After my first job as a florist in a market near my house, I found another creative outlet in gardening– my room quickly filled up with a variety of different plants. Here at Northeastern I've discovered a perfect intersection of my interests in experience design. I've become a competent app designer. I've delved into the world of typography. **I've learned how to code this very website!** To check out some of the work I've done, please click on the links below. To check out my resume click here.
